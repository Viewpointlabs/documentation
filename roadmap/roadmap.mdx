## Roadmap

Viewpoint is built iteratively, with a focus on quality, transparency, and long-term alignment between users and analysts. The roadmap below outlines the key phases and planned expansions.

---

## Phase 1: Launch and foundation

### Open Discord server

- Public Discord server goes live
- Core channels and structure established
- Clear separation between public areas and paid analyst content
- Early community onboarding begins

### Initial payment integration

- Subscriptions enabled via Subscord
- Crypto payments supported (USDC, ERC-20)
- Automatic access control based on subscription tier

### Voting and ranking system

- Mandatory analyst ranking at join and renewal
- Points-based system activated
- Monthly snapshot logic implemented

---

## Phase 2: Analyst onboarding and content expansion

### Onboard founding analysts

- Initial group of high-quality analysts onboarded
- Dedicated analyst desks created
- Clear content and interaction guidelines applied

### Trial and promo access

- Analyst-specific entry codes enabled
- One-month free access for existing analyst communities
- Controlled onboarding without payout distortion

### Content consistency

- Regular briefings, research updates, and discussions established
- Cross-analyst visibility and comparison encouraged

---

## Phase 3: Community growth and engagement

### Platform-led marketing

- Shared exposure across analyst X accounts
- Coordinated announcements and launches
- Organic audience growth through cross-pollination
- Ramp up Viewpoints activity across X 

### Podcasts and X spaces

- Regular Viewpoint-hosted podcasts
- Analyst roundtables and debates
- Live X spaces covering market themes and events

These formats are designed to:

- increase analyst visibility
- attract new users
- deepen engagement beyond written content

---

## Phase 4: Interactive features

### Live chart requests

- Members can submit chart or topic requests
- Requests answered during scheduled sessions or updates
- Focus on explanation and context, not trade execution

### Scheduled live sessions

- Optional live market discussions
- Analyst panels around key macro events
- Structured Q&A

---

## Phase 5: Platform maturity and scaling

### Additional analysts

- Gradual expansion of the analyst desk towards 8 analysts
- Focus on diversity of frameworks and styles
- No uncontrolled analyst sprawl

### Advanced transparency tools

- Improved internal reporting for analysts
- Clear historical comparisons and performance tracking
- Continued emphasis on trust and verification

### Fiat payment options (later stage)

- Introduction of fiat subscription methods
- Seamless coexistence with crypto payments

---

## Long-term vision

Viewpoint aims to become:

- a trusted destination for market understanding
- a scalable distribution platform for analysts
- a sustainable alternative to fragmented private groups

The roadmap is intentionally iterative.\
Features are added when they improve clarity, fairness, and value, not just to increase complexity.
